Who Are We?

Burger shack is a fast food burger joint focused on providing the best quality, fresh, never frozen burgers. With many options for toppings for toppings the combinations are limitless. Customize the way you want it on top of our fresh never frozen patties. And every meal comes with our seasoned fries, seasoned in our top secret house made seasoning. Also try our house made sauce which can be topped on any of our burgers or can be used to dip your fries.

The Beginning

We started the idea for our resturant back when my partner and I were kids and after graduating college we opened our first Burger Shack in Kansas City, KS in 1999. We have been focused on providing the best quality food and service at our single location but want to expand to the greater Kansas City area.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide Kansas City with the freshest food for a low cost. The Overland Park location is the first step into bringing more Burger Shack to the people of Kansas City. We also hope to get our seasonings and sauces into local grocery stores. After expansion through the city we hope to open up Burger Shacks in Kauffmen Stadium, Arrowhead, and Sporting Park.
